Comparing both, the logistic regression model consider: season, age, accident, fever and frequency alcohol consumption. The tree decision consider age, accident, and number of hours spent sitting. Although, the logistic regression consider more variables, the tree decision is a better model to check the probability of diagnostic of fertility or altered.
